Our Client

Ali's Oil

Ali's Oil is a petrol pump based in Sahiwal, Pakistan. Established in 2010, Ali's Oil provides high quality fuel, vehicle related merchandise, as well as an on-site restaurant.

Ali's Oil Homepage

Petrol Station Digitization Project

Ali's Oil currently keeps only paper records. While this method has been sufficient until now, further growth requires a more scalable method of recordkeeping. They hope to achieve this using a digital solution.

FFDS has been chosen to specify requirements for the petrol station digitization project. The final product should be able to track transactions at the station, manage employee data and track customer debts. Completion of the requirements stage of this project is estimated for April 2019.
